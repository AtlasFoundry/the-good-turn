About Us

Built from the heart of Dunoon

The Good Turn is being built by a mother and daughter in Dunoon, with the hope of creating something useful, local and lasting.

Some things begin simply — with a need, a conversation, and two people who care about doing things properly.

The Good Turn was born out of exactly that. A mother and daughter, rooted in Dunoon, who believe that a clean, well-kept home isn't a luxury — it's the foundation of a good day. Whether you're a host welcoming guests to the beauty of Argyll, or a family who simply wants to come home to order and calm, we understand what that means to you. Because we live here too.

We are building this together — deliberately, from the ground up. For us, The Good Turn is more than a cleaning service. It is about showing up reliably for our community, and building something my daughter can grow into, learn from, and one day call her own.

That is the good turn we are here to do. For your home, and for each other.

"The smallest acts of care matter enormously to the people who live in them."
