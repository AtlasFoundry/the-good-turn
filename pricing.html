How we price

Agreed before we begin

A well-cared-for home begins with a clear agreement.

Our standard prices cover the work listed below. Where a property has additional bathrooms, beds or other requirements, these are added separately and shown clearly in the quote.

Once we have confirmed the property and service details, you will receive the full price before the booking is accepted. The only exception is unplanned additional cleaning following a guest stay, which will be photographed and discussed with the host.

  • One bathroom
  • One prepared bed in each advertised bedroom

  • General surfaces dusted and cleaned

Linen belongs to the host

The Good Turn does not supply bedding or towels.

Clean linen must be provided by the property owner and available before the changeover. Hosts must hold enough complete sets to prepare the property for every incoming booking.

An extra bed means any bed beyond the first bed in each advertised bedroom.

For example, a twin room contains one bed within the standard property price and one additional bed charged at £8.

The charge relates to the number of beds being prepared, not only the number of rooms.

Laundry bolt-on

Your linen, washed and ready for next time

Laundry is available as an optional service for bedding and towels owned by the host.

Laundry servicePrice
Wash, dry and fold£18 per standard machine load
Duvets, mattress protectors and other bulky itemsQuoted individually

The £18 charge covers one standard domestic machine load. Bedding and towels may require separate loads.

Laundry collected during a changeover will be returned at the next agreed visit unless another arrangement has been made.

Hosts using this service must keep a duplicate set of clean linen at the property. Laundry collected during a same-day changeover cannot be relied upon for the guests arriving that day.

Linen remains the property of the host. Heavily stained or damaged items will be photographed and reported. Stain removal cannot be guaranteed.

We wash the host's linen. We do not supply or replace it.

After a guest stay

When a property has been left beyond normal guest condition

Standard changeover prices assume the property has been left in normal guest condition.

Excessive rubbish, food waste, staining, dishes, pet hair or soiling may require additional cleaning. We will photograph the issue and contact the host before continuing wherever practical.

Approved additional cleaning is charged at:

£25 per cleaner, per hour

Time is recorded in 30-minute increments after the first hour.

One cleaner working for two hours equals two labour hours. Two cleaners working for one hour also equals two labour hours.

This hourly rate applies only to variable work. Standard changeovers remain fixed-price services.

Reset cleans

A fuller reset

Reset cleans are suitable for:

  • Seasonal properties reopening after a gap
  • Homes requiring more work than a maintenance clean
  • Post-guest recovery
  • Properties being prepared for use, letting or return

Reset cleans start at £125, covering five labour hours.

Further time is charged at £25 per labour hour and agreed before the work continues.

The condition of a reset clean cannot be judged from bedroom count alone. We confirm the likely work after receiving photographs, completing a video walkthrough or visiting the property.

Waste removal, hazardous materials, pest contamination and specialist restoration sit outside the standard reset service. We will say so before accepting the booking if specialist help is more appropriate.
