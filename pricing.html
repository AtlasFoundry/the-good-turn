Every changeover ends with a note like this. For home care a brief message is available on request, and we never photograph a private home unless you have asked.

Linen

The Good Turn washes the host's linen. We do not supply it.

Hosts provide enough clean sets for each arrival. We can wash, dry and fold host-owned linen for £18 per standard machine load.

Laundry collected during a changeover is returned at the next agreed visit, so a same-day changeover cannot rely on it for that day's guests. Linen stays the host's property. Heavily stained or damaged items are photographed and reported, and stain removal cannot be guaranteed.

When a property needs extra care

Standard changeover prices cover a property left in normal guest condition. If a stay leaves more work than expected, we will photograph what we find and contact the host before continuing wherever practical.

For local homes

Home care

£25 per labour hour

Products and equipment are included. Regular weekly or fortnightly cleaning has a two-hour minimum; one-off cleans have a three-hour minimum.

We agree the household's priorities before we begin and work through them within the booked time. A one-off booking does not promise that every area is completed within a fixed number of hours.

A fuller reset

Reset cleans

From £125 for five labour hours

Further time is £25 per labour hour, agreed before it continues. Reset cleans suit seasonal properties reopening after a gap, post-guest recovery, or a space needing more than a maintenance clean.

We confirm the likely work after photographs, a video walkthrough or a visit. Waste removal, hazardous materials, pest contamination and specialist restoration sit outside the standard reset.
